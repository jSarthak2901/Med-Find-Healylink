## Project Overview

Med-Find-HealyLink is a web-based healthcare platform designed to simplify and digitize patient management and therapy scheduling. It provides a centralized system that helps healthcare providers manage patient records, schedule treatments, and monitor progress efficiently.

The platform aims to reduce manual workload while improving accuracy, coordination, and overall patient care through intelligent and automated processes.

---

## Problem Statement

Healthcare systems often rely on manual and fragmented processes, leading to:

- Inefficient appointment scheduling  
- Unorganized patient records  
- Difficulty in tracking therapy progress  
- Poor communication between doctors and patients  
- Increased administrative workload  

---

## Solution

Med-Find-HealyLink offers a digital solution that enables:

- Automated appointment and therapy scheduling  
- Centralized patient data management  
- Dedicated dashboards for doctors and patients  
- AI-based therapy recommendations  
- Notification system for reminders and alerts  

---

## Key Features

- Secure authentication system  
- Doctor dashboard with patient insights and analytics  
- Patient dashboard with appointments and progress tracking  
- Appointment booking, rescheduling, and cancellation  
- Therapy progress monitoring  
- AI-driven recommendations  
- Automated notifications  
- Data visualization and reporting  

---

## Tech Stack

- Frontend: React (Vite), TypeScript  
- UI: Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui  
- Backend and Database: Supabase  
- Tools: Node.js, npm  

---

## Technical Approach

The system follows a structured workflow:

1. Collect patient data such as medical history, vitals, and therapy records  
2. Process the data using intelligent logic and AI-based models  
3. Generate insights and therapy recommendations  
4. Store and visualize data through dashboards  
5. Send notifications for appointments and alerts  

---

## Impact and Benefits

### Core Benefits
- Reduces manual errors and administrative workload  
- Improves therapy adherence and patient engagement  
- Enhances coordination between doctors and patients  

### Broader Impact
- Social: Improved communication and patient care  
- Economic: Reduced operational costs for healthcare providers  
- Technological: Promotes digital transformation in healthcare  
- Environmental: Reduces dependency on paper records
